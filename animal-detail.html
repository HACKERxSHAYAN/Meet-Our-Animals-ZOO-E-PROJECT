About the African Elephant

The African Elephant is the largest land mammal on Earth, known for its massive body, large ears, and remarkable intelligence. These magnificent creatures roam the savannas and forests of Africa in tight-knit family herds led by the oldest female, the matriarch.

At Zoorld, we provide a spacious and naturalistic habitat that allows our elephants to exhibit their natural behaviors. Our conservation program focuses on protecting these gentle giants in the wild and raising awareness about the threats they face from poaching and habitat loss.
